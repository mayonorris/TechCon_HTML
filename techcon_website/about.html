History

Launched in 2010, TechCon began as a small meetup for local developers and engineers. Over the years, it has grown into a global technology conference that welcomes thousands of participants from around the world.

From its first edition in a single auditorium to today’s multi-track, multi-day experience, TechCon has consistently showcased cutting-edge innovations and emerging trends in software, hardware, and digital products.

Mission

The mission of TechCon 2024 is to connect innovators, creators, and learners in order to accelerate responsible technological progress. We aim to create a space where students, professionals, startups, and industry leaders can exchange ideas and build impactful solutions.

Through keynotes, hands-on workshops, and collaborative sessions, TechCon promotes open knowledge-sharing, diversity in tech, and sustainable digital transformation across industries.

Past Speakers

Dr. Amina Chen – AI Researcher

Dr. Chen is a leading researcher in artificial intelligence and ethical machine learning. At TechCon 2021, she delivered a keynote on building transparent and fair AI systems that inspired organizations to rethink how they design and deploy algorithms.

Jamal is a principal cloud architect who has helped large enterprises move securely to the cloud. In his TechCon 2022 talk, he shared practical strategies for designing scalable, resilient architectures for modern applications.

Sofia Ndiaye – Cybersecurity Specialist

Sofia focuses on defending critical infrastructure from cyber threats. During TechCon 2023, she led an interactive session on building a security-first culture within development teams and organizations.
